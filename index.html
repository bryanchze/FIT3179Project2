HAPPINESS

AROUND THE WORLD

Happiness is when one is in a state of well-being and contentment in what they are doing or in life in general. If that is the case, then it comes to no suprise that happiness varies in different countries in the world. The United Nations supplies articles and rankings of the happiness of each country based on the ratings of respondent’s lives correlating with various quality of life factors such as gross domestic product (GDP) per capita, social security, freedom to make choices, generosity of the population, health expectancy and perceptions of corruption.
